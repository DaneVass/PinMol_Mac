Requirements for running PinMol on Mac:

Add the following to the .bash_profile or .profile file:

export PATH="[RNAstructure_path]/RNAstructure/exe:$PATH"

export PATH="[RNAstructure_path]/RNAstructure/exe:$PATH"
export DATAPATH="[RNAstructure_path]/RNAstructure/data_tables/"
